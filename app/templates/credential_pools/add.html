About Credential Pools

Credential pools allow you to group multiple credentials together and try them in order when connecting to devices.

  • Add multiple credentials to a pool
  • Set priority order for trying credentials
  • Assign pools to devices or device groups
  • Automatic failover to next credential on failure

Use Cases:

  • Password rotation scenarios
  • Mixed vendor environments
  • Different access levels (read-only vs admin)
  • Legacy device support
